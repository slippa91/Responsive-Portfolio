About Me

After a successful 21 year career as a Securities Trader with Institutional Capital LLC (ICAP), I find myself with the opportunity to pursue new challanges. Over my career at ICAP I noticed that web-based applications were playing an increasingly important role in the trading process.


Northwestern's Coding Bootcamp provides an immersive environment to explore the latest technologies underpinning web development today. I look forward to a future career either combining the knowledge I have gained in the course with my prior work experience or discovering an entirely new industry.


In my spare time, I enjoy watching Notre Dame women's basketball (Go Irish!), golfing and nature photography. Some pictures from a recent trip to the Seattle area can be seen in the "Project Gallery" section of this website.
